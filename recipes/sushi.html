Sushi Recipe

  • Home

Ingredients (Serves 2-3):

For the Rice:

  • 2 cups sushi rice
  • 2 1/2 cups water
  • 1/4 cup rice vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt

For the Fillings (choose your favorites):

  • 1 cucumber (thinly sliced)
  • 1 avocado (sliced)
  • Fresh fish (like salmon or tuna, sushi-grade)
  • Cooked shrimp or crab sticks
  • Cream cheese (optional)

Other

  • 4-5 sheets of nori (seaweed)
  • Soy sauce (for dipping)
  • Wasabi and pickled ginger (optional, for serving)
  • Bamboo rolling mat (for shaping rolls)
  • Sesame seeds (optional, for garnish)

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare Sushi Rice

  1. Rinse the sushi rice in cold water until the water runs clear.
  2. Cook the rice in 2 1/2 cups of water (using a rice cooker or stovetop). Let it cool slightly.
  3. Mix rice vinegar, sugar, and salt in a small saucepan over low heat until dissolved. Pour the mixture over the warm rice and gently mix. Let it cool.

Step 2: Prepare Fillings

  1. Slice your chosen ingredients (e.g., cucumber, avocado, fish) into thin, long strips.

Step 3: Assemble the Sushi

  1. Place a sheet of nori (shiny side down) on the bamboo mat.
  2. Wet your hands with water to prevent sticking, then spread a thin layer of rice evenly over the nori, leaving about 1 inch (2.5 cm) at the top edge.
  3. Add fillings horizontally across the middle of the rice.
  4. Using the bamboo mat, start rolling the nori tightly from the bottom edge over the fillings. Press gently but firmly as you roll, sealing the edge with a little water.

Step 4: Roll the Sushi

  1. Place a sheet of nori (shiny side down) on the bamboo mat.
  2. Using the bamboo mat, start rolling the nori tightly from the bottom edge over the fillings.
  3. Press gently but firmly as you roll, sealing the edge with a little water.

Step 5: Cut and Serve

  1. Use a sharp knife dipped in water to cut the roll into 6-8 pieces.
  2. Sprinkle with sesame seeds if desired.
  3. Serve with soy sauce, wasabi, and pickled ginger.
